About Me!

I am a first generation college graduate from Binghamton University. With a Bachelor's degree in Finance and Marketing and with my MBA in Management Information Systems and Business Analytics, I have developed a solid foundation in analytical thinking, quantitative analysis, and problem-solving skills that has prepared me for any analyst role.

I'm a finance enthusiast with a keen eye for technology-driven solutions. My journey into the world of finance started during my undergraduate years, where I honed my skills in financial analysis, risk management, and investment strategies. However, it was my fascination with fintech that led me to explore the dynamic intersection of finance and technology.

During my academic journey, I had the privilege of being involved in various organizations such as Alpha Kappa Psi and the Student Association Congress at Binghamton University, where I worked closely with their financial teams. This experience allowed me to witness the interesting world of financial markets and get a little preview.

It wasn't until my senior year where I first messed with Python and realized the limitless potential within it. My first though was to combine it with my financial knowledge and that is when I discovered the world of fintech and my world changed.

This led to me firmly believing that technology can serve as a catalyst for positive change in the financial industry. I am committed to staying at the forefront of fintech innovations, continually learning, and leveraging these advancements to create a positive impact on people's financial lives.

After that I decided to do my MBA right after and improve my skills and knowledge of coding and to see how I can incorporate it into the world of Finance which led to my independent study of how machine learning can predict stock prices on a day to day basis.

I hope you could relate to my story and how I got into Finance and programming and feel free to reach out to me via email at pedrocouto12@outlook.com or connect with me on LinkedIn. I'm always open to discussing fintech, finance, and potential collaboration opportunities.
